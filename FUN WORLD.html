Fun World made a modest start in the early 90s and right from the beginning we have always kept a step forward when it has come to innovation or giving something new to play for. An Amusement park for everyone, we are home to some the best thrill seeking enthusiast for the daredevils, exciting rides for the little fun seekers and rides that the entire family can enjoy. You can enjoy rides like tsunami, rollercoaster, The curse of the black pearl, the Yahooooo slide, the bumper car and lots more.
